INTRODUCTION

Hello! We are young environmental awareness campaign. We are fighting against the trash on the streets in places, where it is not allowed to throw it, but people continues to pollute our environment. All of us want to live in clear surrounding. Our plans for future are big. We are going to set our made by hand bins on the surroundings of Holland ISC and then move on th the whole city. We need the support from government so share our ideas! Then we are moving to supermarkets like Albert Heijn and we will make nice competition where you can win prize! Rules are simple, we will create your account with the help of bonus card and we will count the amount of plastic you are returning to the supermarket. You need just to scan your card while throwing plastic bottles. For every 2 kilo of plastic you will recieve fabric bag and person who will have biggest amount of plastic in account will have our big prise! For further details check our Facebook group. Share our ideas, your support is valuable! And remember that you are already the part of us so let's make our world better place to live!
